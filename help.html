Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I book an artist?

To book an artist, go to the artist's profile page, select the date and time for your event, and click "Book Artist".

What is the cancellation policy?

For cancellations, please contact us at least 48 hours before the event to avoid any charges.

How can I contact customer support?

If you need assistance, you can reach us through the "Contact Us" page or email support@example.com.
